About Jaiti Foundation

Building futures through education, compassion, and unwavering commitment to every child's potential.

Our Beginning

The Story Behind Jaiti Foundation

Jaiti Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their economic circumstances. In the heart of Jaipur, we witnessed countless bright young minds being held back not by lack of ability, but by lack of opportunity.

What started as a small initiative to support a handful of children has grown into a thriving educational community. Today, we proudly serve 60+ students daily and have positively impacted 150+ children's lives since 2024.

Our approach is holistic—we don't just teach academic subjects. We nurture moral values, civic responsibility, and provide nutritious meals, ensuring that every child develops into a well-rounded, confident individual ready to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

To provide completely free, high-quality education and holistic development to underprivileged children in Jaipur, ensuring they have the knowledge, values, and confidence to break the cycle of poverty and build dignified, successful lives.

  • Deliver exceptional academic instruction across all subjects
  • Instill strong moral values and ethical principles
  • Develop civic awareness and social responsibility
  • Provide daily nutritious meals for healthy development
  • Create a safe, nurturing learning environment

Our Vision

A society where every child, regardless of economic background, has equal access to quality education and the opportunity to realize their full potential, creating a ripple effect of positive change across generations.

  • Zero children denied education due to poverty
  • Every student empowered to pursue their dreams
  • Communities transformed through educated youth
  • Breaking intergenerational poverty cycles
  • Building a more equitable, educated society
What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every action we take in service of the children we support.

01

Education as a Right

We firmly believe that quality education is not a privilege for the wealthy but a fundamental right of every child. Economic circumstances should never determine a child's access to learning.

02

Holistic Development

True education extends beyond textbooks. We nurture intellectual growth, moral character, civic responsibility, and physical well-being to develop complete, confident individuals.

03

Dignity & Respect

Every child deserves to be treated with dignity and respect. We create an environment where students feel valued, heard, and empowered, never made to feel inferior due to their circumstances.

04

Transparency & Integrity

We operate with complete honesty and openness in all our activities. Our work is driven by genuine care for children's welfare, not profit or recognition.

05

Community Partnership

We believe in working collaboratively with families and communities, respecting local contexts while providing children with opportunities to break barriers and reach their potential.

06

Long-term Impact

We're not interested in quick fixes. Our goal is to create lasting, transformational change that impacts not just individual children, but entire families and future generations.

Our Approach

What Makes Jaiti Foundation Different

100% Free Services

Every single service we provide—education, materials, meals—is completely free. No hidden costs, no fees, no barriers to access.

Holistic Curriculum

Beyond academics, we integrate moral education, civic sense, life skills, and character development into our daily teaching.

Daily Nutritious Meals

Understanding that hungry children cannot learn effectively, we provide healthy, balanced meals every day to support physical and cognitive development.

Personal Attention

Small class sizes and dedicated educators ensure every child receives individual attention and support tailored to their unique learning needs.
