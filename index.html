Our Purpose

Why Jaiti Foundation Exists

Jaiti Foundation works alongside schools, communities, volunteers and mentors to include, educate, and empower every child in India's slum communities.

Breaking the Cycle

We work to break the intergenerational cycle of poverty through education, giving children tools to create better futures for themselves and their families.

Education is a Right

We believe education is not a privilege reserved for the wealthy, but a fundamental right that every child deserves, regardless of their economic background.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we nurture moral values, civic responsibility, and provide nutritious meals, ensuring complete development of each child.

Our Philosophy

Education Changes Everything

At Jaiti Foundation, we don't just teach subjects—we transform lives. Our holistic approach combines academic excellence with character building, ensuring that every child not only learns to read and write, but also develops the values and skills needed to thrive in life.

We provide a safe, nurturing environment where underprivileged children can discover their potential, dream big, and build the confidence to achieve those dreams. Through consistent support, nutritious meals, and caring mentorship, we're creating a generation of educated, empowered, and responsible citizens.
