Get Involved With Us

Your time, skills, and passion can help transform the lives of children who dream of a better future.

Why Your Support Matters

Be the Change You Want to See

Every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and succeed. But many lack access to basic education due to poverty. Your involvement—whether through teaching, mentoring, or supporting in other ways—can break this cycle and give children the foundation they need for a brighter future.

Direct Impact

See the tangible difference your efforts make in children's lives every single day

Community Connection

Join a passionate community of educators and changemakers working toward a common goal

Personal Growth

Develop leadership, teaching, and life skills while making meaningful contributions

How You Can Help

Ways to Contribute

Whether you have a few hours a week or specific skills to share, there's a meaningful way for you to contribute.

01

Teaching & Tutoring

Help children with academic subjects, homework, or exam preparation. Share your knowledge in mathematics, science, languages, or any other subject you're passionate about.

  • Regular teaching sessions
  • Subject-specific tutoring
  • Homework assistance
  • Exam preparation support
02

Mentorship & Guidance

Be a role model and guide for children navigating challenges. Help them build confidence, set goals, and develop a positive outlook on their future possibilities.

  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Life skills workshops
  • Personal development support
03

Skills & Workshops

Conduct workshops on specialized topics like art, music, sports, computers, environmental awareness, or any skill that can enrich children's learning experience.

  • Creative arts classes
  • Sports and fitness activities
  • Technology workshops
  • Cultural programs
04

Administrative Support

Help with organizing activities, managing schedules, documentation, social media, or other operational tasks that keep our programs running smoothly.

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Documentation and record keeping
  • Social media management
  • Resource coordination
05

Resource Contribution

Provide educational materials, books, stationery, or help source resources that support children's learning and development needs.

  • Educational materials
  • Books and stationery
  • Learning aids and tools
  • Infrastructure improvements
06

Partnerships & Collaboration

Connect us with organizations, institutions, or professionals who can support our mission through collaboration, expertise, or resources.

  • Corporate partnerships
  • Educational institutions
  • Professional networks
  • Community organizations
What We Look For

Volunteer Requirements

We welcome individuals from all backgrounds who are passionate about education and children's welfare. Here's what makes a great volunteer at Jaiti Foundation:

Commitment & Reliability

Children thrive on consistency. We seek volunteers who can commit to regular, scheduled involvement.

Patience & Empathy

Understanding that children come from challenging backgrounds and may need extra patience and care.

Passion for Education

Genuine belief in the transformative power of education and desire to make a difference.

Respectful Approach

Treating every child with dignity and respect, never making them feel inferior due to circumstances.

Team Player

Willingness to collaborate with other volunteers and staff toward common goals.

No formal teaching qualifications required! What matters most is your dedication, compassion, and willingness to contribute meaningfully to these children's lives.
